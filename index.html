My curriculum vitae.

Research interests: Probability theory, particularly the asymptotic behaviors of large interacting particle systems, and the interplay among partial differential equations (PDEs), stochastic PDEs, and integrable probability.

My research is supported by the Sloan Research Fellowship, and by the National Science Foundation (NSF; DMS-1953407, DMS-1712575). I was a 2020 Bernoulli Society New Researcher Awardee.

I am currently an Associated Editor of the Journal of Statistical Physics. Before joining Rutgers, I was a Junior Fellow with the Simons Society of Fellows at Columbia University from 2016 to 2019. I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Stanford University in 2016.
