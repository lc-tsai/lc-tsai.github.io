My curriculum vitae.

I am a fifth-year PhD student in Mathematics at Stanford University, and currently a visiting KITP Graduate Fellow at UC Santa Barbara.

My PhD advisor is Professor Amir Dembo. My research interest is in probability theory, in particular the asymptotic fluctuation of interacting particle systems. Before joining Stanford, I worked on the kinetic theory of gas with Professor Tai-Ping Liu.
